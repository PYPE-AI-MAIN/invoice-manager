About Categories

Categories help you organize your invoices by type:

  • Invoices are automatically categorized based on the sender's domain
  • Common categories include:
    • Cloud Services
    • Software
    • Hardware
    • Travel
    • Payment Services
    • And more...
  • Categories are used in monthly reports to group expenses

You can filter invoices by category on the Invoices page.
